BUSMAN 4.0

Internationally known under the Rhythm and Blues moniker, Uniquesoul, an established Hip Hip artist and performer in Shanghai, is known for playing with some of the best musicians and emcees in China.

Uniquesoal was born in Washington, D.C. and lived most of his adult life in Columbus, Ohio. During his 10 years in the Midwest he enjoyed life, started a family and soon after decided to pursue his musical and artistic endeavors as a singer songwriter.

Since then, he has moved to Shanghai, China (2013) where he has built a recognizable name for himself as a leading entertainer within the new Hip Hop and Rhythm and Blues community that he himself helped create and nurture in the city. In the past he has produced or contributed to several staple events and collaborations including Iron Mic, Come Correct, Ninja Radio and The Movement, now The Foundation. With the addition of new talents who come into the city they now have many outlets and venue opportunities to do what they love to do: make music and share it with the world.

Distinctly different from Uniquesoul stands a charismatic Busman 4.0, a gritty alter ego to his softer side. Busman is currently, working on the completion of his latest album 'ONE', anticipating his new music video and the release of his tell all book 'I'm crawling, but I'm almost there'. Truly a gifted artist, Busman also regularity intrigues the city with colorful, hand painted art. As the end of 2018 approaches, you can expect to hear a lot more from this artist in music, art and fashion. Stay tuned for his feature performance at Shanghai's Black Fashion Week and an installation of his latest art series.
